About This Blog

This blog is devoted to showing my appreciation for a variety of Typo products. Belonging to Australia's biggest international retailer Cotton On, Typo is an office supplies brand selling quirky stationery, up-to-date presents and plenty of supercool homeware.

Since the first Melbourne COVID lockdown in 2020, I have had more free time on my hands, and much of that time is spent on surfing and online shopping (Can anyone relate to this?). I then found out that besides birthday cards and colour pencils, Typo offers many DIY kits, tech accessories and bizarre games.
