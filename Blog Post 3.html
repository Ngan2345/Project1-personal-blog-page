Typo Pocket Pal & Brick Gamer

"These two pocket gamers bring back the retro vibes"

My hobby of collecting cute items date back to my highschool time, when my school was not far away from an accessories shop. I remember staying an hour in that shop just looking at different types of notebooks, planners, cards and bookmarks. Now that there are even a wider variety of stationeries, such as water bottle, powerbanks and DIY kits, I surely can stay longer than that.

I am also a book collector, but quite a slow reader.
